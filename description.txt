✧ Terramon is a cross between Terraria and the awesome Pokémon franchise. The mod adds many Pokémon and your mission is to catch 'em all! Featuring 145+ diverse Pokémon to catch with a wide range of different Poké Balls, Terramon is the perfect mod to spice up your playthrough.

✧ Currently in the mod, there's...
• Over 100 Pokémon!
• Full Pokemon Battles!
• New UI and mechanics!
• Various decorations!
